Bengal Tiger

Bengal tigers can ingest up to 40 kilograms of food on a single occasion, although they usually consume a smaller amount.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Body Weight: 140 - 230 kg
  • Body Height: 240 - 310 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 8 to 10 years

The Bengal Tiger is the most numerous of the tiger subspecies. About 3 890 tigers now live in 13 countries across Asia. It has yellow to orange coat with stripes ranging from dark brown to black. The white tiger is a recessive mutant of the Bengal Tiger.
